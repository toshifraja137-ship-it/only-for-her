Hey, Agar tum ye padh rahi ho, shayad abhi tum khud ko thoda akela feel kar rahi ho. Shayad tumhe lag raha ho ki koi samajh nahi raha, ya shayad tum bas kisi ki presence miss kar rahi ho. Main bas tumhe ek baat yaad dilana chahta hoon. Tum akeli nahi ho. Ho sakta hai main abhi tumhare paas physically na hoon, ho sakta hai hum ek dusre se door ho, lekin iska matlab ye nahi ki tum akeli ho. Mere din ka ek hissa hamesha tumhare saath rehta hai. Main kabhi kabhi random moments me bhi tumhare baare me sochta hoon aur sochta hoon ki tum theek ho na. Jab bhi tumhe lage ki tumhare paas koi nahi hai, to ye yaad rakhna ki duniya ke kisi kone me ek ladka hai jo tumhari fikr karta hai, tumhari khushi ke liye dua karta hai, aur chahta hai ki tum hamesha muskuraati raho. Mujhe pata hai ki kuch din bahut mushkil hote hain. Kuch raaton me dil zyada udaas ho jata hai. Kuch waqt aisa lagta hai jaise sab kuch bahut heavy ho gaya ho. Lekin tumhe sab kuch akela nahi uthana hai. Agar tum thak jao, to thoda rest kar lena. Agar rona ho, to ro lena. Agar kisi ki yaad aaye, to usse yaad kar lena. Lekin kabhi ye mat sochna ki tum akeli ho. Aur agar main is waqt tumhare paas hota, to shayad bas itna kehta:
